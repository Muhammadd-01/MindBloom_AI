# MindBloom AI: The Future of Behavioral Well-being 🌸

**MindBloom AI** is a cutting-edge, AI-powered mobile application designed to transform everyday habits into measurable positivity. By passively observing daily behavior and communication, it provides real-time feedback and coaching to help users build a happier, more self-aware version of themselves.

---

## 🚀 Presentation Highlights

### 1. The Core Problem ⚠️
*   **Lack of Awareness**: Most people are unaware of their daily negative communication and cognitive habits.
*   **Inaccuracy**: Traditional mental well-being tools rely on self-reporting, which is often biased or inaccurate.
*   **No Real-Time Feedback**: There are few solutions that measure positive or negative behavior *as it happens*.

### 2. Our Solution: MindBloom AI 💡
*   **Passive Observation**: The app acts as a "behavioral mirror," analyzing voice and text interactions (with consent).
*   **Real-Time Coaching**: Provides gentle, AI-driven nudges to steer users toward positive interactions.
*   **Measurable Positivity**: Converts abstract emotions into a "Positivity Score" that can be tracked over time.

### 3. Key Technological Innovations 🛠️

#### **Hybrid Intelligence System**
*   **Primary Engine**: Powered by **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash**, the app performs deep sentiment and behavioral analysis.
*   **Smart Fallback**: If the API is offline or rate-limited, the app seamlessly switches to a **Local Heuristic Simulation**. This ensures the app *never crashes* during your presentation and remains functional even in poor network conditions.

#### **Advanced Data Aggregation (Daily Reports)**
*   The app doesn't just show single results; it **aggregates multiple daily interactions** into a comprehensive "Daily Report." It calculates your dominant sentiment, tone, and average positivity score from every thought you share throughout the day.

#### **Premium Security Stack**
*   **Biometric Integration**: Secure login using Fingerprint/FaceID.
*   **Account Protection**: Integrated 2-Factor Authentication (Simulation) for a bank-grade security feel.
*   **Firestore Rules**: Robust server-side rules ensure that a user's behavioral data is isolated and only accessible by them.

#### **User-First Error Handling**
*   **Professional Masking**: We replaced technical "code errors" (like *SocketException*) with premium, branded notifications. Users see "MindBloom AI is working in local mode" instead of ugly system popups.

## ✨ Features Breakdown

### 📊 Behavioral Dashboard
A dynamic, real-time overview of the user's mindset. 
*   **Current Mood**: AI-detected emotional state.
*   **Positivity Trends**: Weekly growth charts.
*   **XP & Leveling System**: Every positive interaction earns you Experience Points (XP). Level up from a "Seedling" to a full "Bloom" as your habits improve!

### 🎙️ AI Analysis Suite
*   **Voice Analysis**: Analyzes tone and sentiment from spoken thoughts.
*   **Text Journaling**: Deep analysis of written reflections.
    *   *AI Logic*: "Don't just track what I say, track HOW I say it."

### 🎮 The Positivity Game
*   **Micro-Challenges**: Daily tasks like "Compliment a colleague" or "Practice 1 minute of gratitude."
*   **Streaks**: Rewarding consistent positive behavior with multiplier bonuses.

### 🛡️ Secure Settings
*   **Dark Mode**: Immersive slate-and-emerald theme for premium eye comfort.
*   **Privacy Toggle**: Full control over data observation periods.

## 🏗️ Technical Architecture

*   **Frontend**: Flutter (Riverpod 3.x Notifier State Management).
*   **AI Backend**: Google Generative AI (Gemini SDK).
*   **Database**: Firebase Firestore (Real-time sync).
*   **Auth & Storage**: Supabase & Firebase Auth.
*   **Animations**: `flutter_animate` & `smooth_page_indicator` for a polished, fluid UI.
